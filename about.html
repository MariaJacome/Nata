About Nat

Creative art director based in Madrid

Specialized in Art Direction for Fashion and Styling through the University of Arts London. With more than 6 years of experience in advertising and currently Head of Art at Havas Creative. Throughout my career I have worked for national and international brands such as Vogue, Glamour, GQ, Yves Saint Laurent, Disney +, Solán de Cabras & Beefeater among many others.
